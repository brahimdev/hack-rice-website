Traveling to HackRice

If you've been accepted to HackRice from out of Rice, here is what you need to know about planning your travel. We're very excited to see you here!

Note that all reimbursements are for round trips only. For all reimbursements, we need your address and itemized reciept of travel; details on how to submit this information will be sent to you at a later time. Your team must submit a project to Devpost for you to be eligible for reimbursement.

Logistics

Date + Time

You should look to arrive at Rice at 5:00 PM, October 14th for check-in. The hackathon ends at 3:00 PM, October 16th, so please keep that in mind when scheduling your return flights.

Getting Here

Fly in to either IAH or HOU. You can then take Bus 73 and transfer onto the MetroRail at Fannin South to get to Rice from HOU, or Bus 102 and transfer onto the MetroRail at Downtown Transit Center to get to Rice from IAH. Alternatively, Ubers are available and will cost about $30.

If you plan on being reimbursed, you will need to bring a physical copy of your receipt or email us a scanned copy before October 31st.

HackRice is located at McNair Hall, Rice University, Houston, TX (also known as the Jones Graduate School of Business). Rice's address is 6100 Main Street, Houston, TX 77005.

Flights

We'll be reimbursing based off your round trip flight cost.

Here's some tips: We found Los Angeles flights to be significantly cheaper than San Diego, we found Sacramento flights to be significantly cheaper than San Jose or San Fransisco, and we found Newark flights to be significantly cheaper than NYC. We also reccomend flying on discount airlines when possible; Houston Hobby airport is usually cheaper than Bush Intercontinental.

Buses

This year we will not be providing buses to schools; if you choose to take a bus (Greyhound, Megabus, etc.), then we can reimburse up to $40 of your fare.

Driving + Parking

Grab your friends! To be reimbursed for driving, your car needs to have at least two attendees. We'll reimburse 20 cents per mile, up to a total of $90 per car. Parking is available at Greenbriar Lot, behind the stadium, for $2 per entry per 24 hours (must use credit card). Check out Rice's campus map .
